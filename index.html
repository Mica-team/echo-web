About Echo




Echo is an open-source AI companion robot designed to be more than just a machine. It is being developed to move, interact, assist, and learn alongside its user. Combining robotics, artificial intelligence, and modern software, Echo aims to become a personal companion capable of understanding commands, communicating naturally, and performing useful tasks.





The project is built around the ESP32 platform and is paired with a dedicated mobile application for wireless control, monitoring, and future AI-powered features. As development continues, Echo will gain advanced capabilities including voice interaction, autonomous navigation, computer vision, environmental awareness, and smart home integration.





Echo is currently under active development as a long-term project. Every version brings new hardware improvements, smarter software, and more intelligent behaviors. The ultimate goal is to create an affordable, open, and customizable AI robot that anyone can build, modify, and improve.
