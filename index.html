About Echo


        

            Echo is an open-source AI companion robot designed to move,
            interact, assist, and learn alongside its user.
        


        

            By combining robotics, artificial intelligence, and modern
            software, Echo aims to become an affordable and customizable
            companion capable of helping people in everyday life.
